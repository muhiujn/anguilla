About Us

Discover our story, mission, and the people behind Anguilla Animal Rescue Foundation

Our Mission

Committed to Animal Welfare

At Anguilla Animal Rescue Foundation, our mission is to rescue and care for abandoned and surrendered animals, providing them with the necessary medical care, socialization, and love they deserve. We strive to be a beacon of compassion, offering resources and support to our community and fostering a culture of responsible pet ownership.

Our Vision

A World Where Every Animal is Loved and Cared For

We envision a world where every animal has the opportunity to live in a loving and caring home. Our goal is to be the premier destination for animal welfare, fostering an environment where individuals can connect, learn, and serve together to ensure the well-being of all animals.

Our Values

Guiding Principles That Shape Our Service

Animal-Centered

We place the well-being of animals at the core of everything we do, ensuring that our resources and services align with the highest standards of animal care.

Community-Focused

We believe in the power of community and strive to create a welcoming, inclusive environment for all who seek to support and care for animals in need.

Continuous Learning

We are committed to lifelong learning and growth, constantly seeking new ways to enrich our resources and services to meet the evolving needs of our community and the animals we serve.

Our History

A Journey of Compassion and Service

  • 2012

    Humble Beginnings

    Anguilla Animal Rescue Foundation started as a small initiative to rescue and care for abandoned animals in the community.

  • 2015

    Expansion and Growth

    As demand grew, we expanded our services and resources to reach a wider community, including medical care and adoption programs.

  • 2023

    Digital Transformation

    In response to global challenges, we launched our online platform, making our resources accessible to people around the world.

  • Today

    Serving with Purpose

    We continue to grow and adapt, always focused on our mission to rescue and care for animals in need.
