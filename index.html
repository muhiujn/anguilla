About the Anguilla Animal Rescue Foundation

Our mission is to rescue and care for animals in need.

The Anguilla Animal Rescue Foundation (AARF) was established in 2012 with the goal of providing rescue and care for abandoned and surrendered animals. We believe that every animal deserves a loving home and proper care.

Our foundation offers a wide range of services, including medical care, socialization, and adoption programs. We also engage with the local community, educating individuals about responsible pet ownership and humane treatment of animals.

As a dedicated nonprofit organization operating in The Valley, Anguilla, we are committed to fostering a culture of compassion and responsible pet ownership. Our work is grounded in the belief that every animal deserves a chance at a happy and healthy life.

One of our core programs focuses on providing support to families and individuals who are unable to care for their pets. We understand the challenges they face and aim to provide practical assistance and resources.
